Robotics Team

As a team, we are Neil McNeil High School's First Robotics Competition team, proudly representing ourselves as number 6975. We made our debut in 2018 as a rookie team and have since worked hard to establish ourselves as a formidable force in the competition. Our team is comprised of talented and driven high school students who are passionate about robotics and employ innovative strategies in software development, mechanical building, and electrical planning to design and construct our robots.

  • Collaboration and teamwork are the cornerstones of our team's success, and we place a strong emphasis on creating a supportive and inclusive environment that encourages creativity and problem-solving.
  • Our reputation for excellence has grown steadily since our inception, and we look forward to continuing to compete and excel in future First Robotics Competitions.

Things We Do

CAD Modeling

CAD Design enables designers to see the 3D robotic model from various angles and check if they align with the geometric parameters provided

Electrical Circuit Design

Most robots have a lot of these types of built-in electronic circuits that make decisions and responses

Programming - Java

Java and its numerous high-level features have become an essential programming language for robotics programming

Robot Construction

A basic typical robot has a movable physical structure, a motor of some sort, a sensor system, a power supply and a computer "brain" that controls all of these elements.

Research

We are dedicated to advancing the field of robotics through cutting-edge research. Our team is committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible through ongoing experimentation, data analysis, and innovation.

Robo Maneuvers

Our drive team members are trained in driving techniques and have extensive experience operating our robotic systems in a variety of challenging environments. Whether we are navigating complex obstacle courses or performing delicate maneuvers in tight spaces, we bring a level of skill and precision to our driving.
